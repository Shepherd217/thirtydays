The Ignorance Tax
If you don't file an 83(b) election within 30 days of your grant date, the IRS will tax you on the full fair market value at vest — not the price you paid. On a $1M vest, that could mean $370,000 extra in taxes. The IRS doesn't accept late filings. No exceptions.
