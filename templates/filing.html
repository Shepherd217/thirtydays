Step 2: Download IRS Form 8319
The official IRS form for 83(b) elections

Form 8319 is the election form. Print it, fill it by hand (or type directly), sign it in blue ink.

📄 Download Form 8319 from IRS

The form is simple. Part I is about you (name, address, SSN). Part II is about the property (your grant details). Part III is the actual election statement. Sign and date the bottom.

⚠ Use Certified Mail with Return Receipt
This costs about $4 but creates a legal record that the IRS received your election. The return receipt is your proof. Don't skip this. The IRS doesn't notify you when they receive 83(b) elections.
